Gingerbread Cutout Cookies

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup of butter
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup of molasses
  • 2 teaspoons of ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 3/4 teaspoon of ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • Vanilla frosting
  • Red and green past food coloring

    • Directions

  • In a large bowl, cream the butter and the brown sugar until light and fluffy. Add egg and molasses. Combine the flour, ginger, baking soda, cinnamon, cloves, and salt. Add to creamed mixture and mix well. Cover and regfrigerate for 4 hours.
  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees. On a lightly floured surface, roll dough to 1/8 inch thickness. Cut with floured 2 inches. Use cookie cutters. Place 1 inch apart on ungreased baking sheets.
  • Bake until edges are firm or about 10 minutes. Remove to wire racks to cool. Tint some of the frosting red and some green.Decorate cookies!
